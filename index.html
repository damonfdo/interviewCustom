Explore Our Services

  • 01 Data Storage

    Origen offers enterprise-grade storage solutions. Our solutions adapt to your growing environments, fit into your budget and ensure data is available and performs when you need it

  • 02 Hosted VoIP PBX

    Origen has experience with IP PBX for over 10 years now. Change the way you do business with our Hosted PBX and benefit from high-end VoIP features.

  • 03Endpoint Devices

    Empower your workforce with a rich and smooth experience with all your endpoint systems and devices. Leverage the cumulative effect of having great hardware and software across the board, allowing each user in your organization to further contribute via productivity.

  • 04Business Internet

    Origen offers a high availability and dedicated Fiber Internet Connection for mid to large enterprises delivered via fiber optic. Connection is backed by high quality bandwidth and exceptional customer service.

  • 05Networking

    Improve how your data flows, have your applications connect with optimized and fluid networking. Whether it’s for storage systems, applications or connectivity, networking is a key part your infrastructures performance.

  • 06Compute and Hyperconverged

    Drive your business forward with infrastructure that matches your requirements. Reduce costs and strain by capitalizing in an environment that handles today's workload and expands to accommodate future growth.
